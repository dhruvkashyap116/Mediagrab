Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service completely free?
Yes, MediaGrab is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or download limits.
What websites are supported?
We support downloading from YouTube, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Facebook, Vimeo, and over 1000 other websites.
How long are downloaded files kept?
For privacy and storage reasons, all downloaded files are automatically deleted from our servers 10 minutes after the download completes. Make sure to save your file locally!
Why did my YouTube video fail to download?
If a video is age-restricted, members-only, or private, you will need to upload a cookies.txt file using the banner to authenticate your request.

Privacy Policy

Your Data is Safe and Secure

Welcome to MediaGrab. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Data Collection: We do not collect or store any personal data. The URLs you paste are processed purely to fetch media content and are not associated with any personal identifier.

2. File Storage: Any file you download is temporarily processed on our servers and automatically deleted 10 minutes after generation for security and privacy reasons. We retain NO copies of your files.

3. Local History: Your download history is stored entirely locally on your browser using localStorage. It is completely private to you. Items are automatically removed after 30 minutes, or you can clear them manually.

4. Third-Party Services: We use standard libraries like yt-dlp to process media, which interacts with third-party platforms on your behalf. We do not track your behavior across these platforms.
